# Anbeeld's Custom AI

Anbeeld's Custom AI is a Stellaris 2.1.3 AI overhaul mod.

It replaces large parts of the vanilla AI decision layer with custom scheduling, economy logic, building management, ship production, starbase planning, diplomacy retuning, and megastructure priorities. The goal is a more coherent empire AI that develops its economy and military with fewer obvious vanilla failures.
